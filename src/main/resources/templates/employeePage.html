Employee Page

Welcome to the Employee Page, where you can create new elements to store in the Database.
These elements comprise Services, Optional Products, Validity Periods and Service Packages.
On the bottom of the page you can find two buttons:
one allows to open the Inspection Page, where you can see some information about purchases, while the other allows you to log-out from the application.
Service Package creation will take place in another page, in order to enhance the application's ease of use.
